Overview

Purpose

This website will be used to promote agro-business through web-based e-marketplace between rural and urban agro-business owners. It will provide information to rural farmers about buyers of their agricultural produce in urban cities and international- exports. LIDI will provide help and empower the poor farmers with microfinance, and make payment of their goods easy through the use of the e-marketplace application. It will in addition, do marketing and advertising for the rural farmers

Audience

Livelihood Development Institute's website is for rural farmers, and how they will sell and connect their produce to urban buyers through the e-marketplace.. Rural farners and urban agro-business as well as international market will access to this web page and application in order to make business transaction easier by using LIDI' pay application.]

Content

Livelihood Delivelopment Institute

Livelihood Development Institute (LIDI) is a web-based agro-business marketplace that is helping rural farmers to sell their products to urban and international buyers, who sell and process agro-food. It is the only agro-technological website that helps to promote agriculture activities in Liberia. The website is used by farmers and agro-busiesses to connect their transactions. Through LIDI, farmers are earning high profits and local agro industry is adding value to agrocultural produce. thus the country is earning foregn currencies to improve the economy. LIDI values the environment, as a result it is making continuous efforts in promoting sustainable agriculture. ]
