Making Trade and Payment

Farmers and buyers can use the web-based application to transact. Also, LIDI has application that can be used on mobile phone to buy agro-products. The website makes payement easier and faster.

Trade With Us

Buyer are informed when the products are ready to be sold. The e-marketplace is used to encourage trade across Liberia and to rest of the world. Since LIDI came to being, Agro-trade has increased in the country and is made easy for both buyers and sellers.
